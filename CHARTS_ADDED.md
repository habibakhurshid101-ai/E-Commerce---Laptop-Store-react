# ✅ Charts Added to Dashboard!

## What's Added

### 4 Interactive Charts ✅

1. **📊 Monthly Sales Volume** (Bar Chart)
   - Shows units sold per month
   - Last 6 months data
   - Blue bars

2. **🍩 Stock by Category** (Doughnut Chart)
   - Laptops, Desktops, Gaming, Accessories, Workstations
   - Color-coded segments
   - Shows inventory distribution

3. **📈 Revenue Trend** (Line Chart)
   - Monthly revenue in PKR
   - Last 6 months
   - Green line with fill

4. **🥧 Stock Status** (Pie Chart)
   - In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock
   - Color-coded: Green, Amber, Red
   - Shows stock health

## Technology Used

### Chart.js + React-Chartjs-2
```bash

```

**Benefits:**

- ✅ Interactive (hover to see values)
- ✅ Smooth animations

- ✅ TypeScript support
